Assignment

What becomes visible when you look beneath the final product?

Most assignments are designed from the outside in — what students will submit, when it is due, how it will be graded. But students experience assignments from the inside out: a sequence of cognitive moves, decisions, and moments of confusion that happen long before anything is turned in, and learning that continues long after.

This tool surfaces that hidden structure. Select an assignment type and take an X-ray to see what is actually going on beneath the surface.
